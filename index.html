In Bubble Sort, the method works by examining each set of adjacent elements in the string, from left to right, switching their positions if they are out of order.

In Selection Sort, it selects the smallest element in the array and swaps it into place.

Insertion sort works by continually inserting each element from an unsorted subarray into a sorted subarray.

Quicksort is a divide-and-conquer algorithm. It works by selecting a 'pivot' element from the array and partitioning the other elements into two sub-arrays, according to whether they are less than or greater than the pivot.
